# AI Back Office

## Definition

The idea of delegating operational/administrative tasks — email triage, scheduling, volunteer rosters, marketing, communication — to AI agents, freeing humans in mission-driven organizations to focus on people-facing work. Coined internally at [[concepts/human-good-principle|Human Good AI]] as "less admin, more good."

## Key Characteristics

- Target customers: nonprofits, charter schools, mission-driven orgs with lean teams
- Must be "dead simple" — accessible to non-technical staff
- Covers: email prioritization, volunteer roster management, substitute teacher reminders, marketing drafts, newsletter creation
- Does not replace human relationships — it makes more time for them
- "Agentic office in a box" — a single deployable system covering multiple back-office functions

## Applications

- **Food bank:** delivery coordination, volunteer list management
- **School:** substitute reminders, parent email triage, newsletter drafts, schedule management
- **Nonprofit marketing:** agent that takes voice note + photos → draft newsletter or email → human review → publish
