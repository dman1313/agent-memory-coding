# ATLs

## Definition

Approaches to Learning — the IB's framework of transdisciplinary skills (thinking, communication, social, self-management, research) assessed alongside academic content in the [[concepts/ib-pyp|IB PYP]] and other IB programmes. Also linked to the IB Learner Profile attributes (inquirer, thinker, communicator, etc.).

## Key Characteristics

- Assessed in parallel with academic content
- Used at [[entities/ics-paris|ICS Paris]] for student reporting and planning
- Connected to how teachers document their practice on [[concepts/totl|TOTL]]
