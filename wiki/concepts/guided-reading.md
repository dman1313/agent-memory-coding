# Guided Reading

## Definition

A structured small-group literacy instructional approach used at [[entities/ics-paris|ICS Paris]], where teachers work with groups of students at similar reading levels. Connected to running records assessment, levelled texts, and the broader literacy curriculum development work Dwayne was leading in 2024–2025.

## Key Characteristics

- Requires a dedicated reading program and librarian support
- Running records used for assessment (literacy benchmarking)
- Challenged by lack of a strong librarian and resource organisation issues
- Integrated with writing curriculum development
- Aligns with IB PYP through the [[concepts/ib-pyp|IB PYP]] units of inquiry
