# TOTL

## Definition

A digital curriculum planning platform used at [[entities/ics-paris|ICS Paris]] for documenting units of inquiry, lesson plans, and collaboration records within the [[concepts/ib-pyp|IB PYP]] framework. Teachers are required to plan and document on TOTL; [[entities/dwayne-primeau|Dwayne Primeau]] used it as a compliance and transparency mechanism.

## Key Characteristics

- Plans remain stored permanently ("it stays there forever")
- Specialist teachers (like [[entities/lucy|Lucy]]) were required to use it for lesson documentation
- Can be used to create templates to standardise planning
- Central to collaboration between homeroom and specialist teachers
