# IB PYP

## Definition

The International Baccalaureate Primary Years Programme — an inquiry-based framework for students aged 3–12. At [[entities/ics-paris|ICS Paris]], it governs curriculum planning, collaboration meetings (CPMs), transdisciplinary units of inquiry (UOIs), learner profile attributes, and Approaches to Learning (ATLs).

## Key Characteristics

- Structured around transdisciplinary Units of Inquiry with central ideas and lines of inquiry
- Requires collaboration between specialist teachers (art, music, PE) and homeroom teachers
- Uses planners (TOTL) for unit documentation
- ATLs (Approaches to Learning) and Learner Profile attributes are assessed and tracked
- PASS test used for grades 2–5 assessment

## Applications

- Curriculum planning meetings (CPMs) at [[entities/ics-paris|ICS Paris]]
- Art teacher collaboration requirements with homeroom teachers
- Literacy/guided reading within the UOI framework
