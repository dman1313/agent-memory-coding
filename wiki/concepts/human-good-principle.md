# Human Good Principle

## Definition

The belief that technology — specifically AI — should be directed toward human benefit: connecting people, reducing meaningless labour, and freeing capacity for mission-driven work. Named after [[concepts/human-good-principle|Human Good AI]] but articulated as a broader personal philosophy by [[entities/dwayne-primeau|Dwayne Primeau]].

## Key Characteristics

- Humans are hardwired to cooperate; technology should amplify that, not replace it
- "Giving back" is a duty for those who have benefited from education and opportunity
- AI should handle the tasks "we don't really enjoy" so humans can spend more time connecting
- Kindness repays kindness; doing good creates positive feedback loops
- Profit is a means to fund impact, not the end goal

## Applications

- Founding mission of [[concepts/human-good-principle|Human Good AI]]
- Design principle for [[concepts/ai-back-office|AI Back Office]]: automate admin, maximise human connection time
- Personal ethic articulated in voice notes (2026-05-12)

# Human Good AI

## Basic Information

- **Type:** organization (startup / social enterprise)
- **Founder:** [[entities/dwayne-primeau|Dwayne Primeau]]
- **Stage:** early — solo founder, products in trial

## Description

Human Good AI is a startup founded by [[entities/dwayne-primeau|Dwayne Primeau]] with the mission of using AI agents to support nonprofits, charter schools, and mission-driven organizations — freeing humans from back-office admin so they can focus on meaningful work. The founding principle: "less admin, more good." The org grew from a recognition that nonprofits and schools spend significant budget on operations (marketing, scheduling, email triage, volunteer management) that agentic AI can handle. First product is [[entities/agent-ready|Agent Ready]], an agent-friendly website layer. A longer-term goal is an "office in a box" for schools and nonprofits. Financially, Human Good plans to launch paid products first to fund goodwill/impact projects, alongside grant and token-donation fundraising. As of 2026-06, it is a solo operation with informal collaborators.
