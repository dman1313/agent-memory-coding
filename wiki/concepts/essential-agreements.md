# Essential Agreements

## Definition

A formal set of agreed expectations established between school leadership and a staff member (or team), typically used when informal coaching has not produced the required behaviour change. At [[entities/ics-paris|ICS Paris]], essential agreements were used as a structured step in managing [[entities/lucy|Lucy]]'s non-compliance with collaboration and lesson-planning requirements.

## Key Characteristics

- Written, specific, and time-bound commitments
- Followed by a support/coaching offer if not met ("how can we support you?")
- Non-compliance documented via email and tracked over time
- Part of a progressive management approach: essential agreements → coaching → formal action

## Applications

- [[entities/lucy|Lucy]] art class: lesson plans 24h in advance, cleanup responsibilities, collaboration meetings
- General staff management at [[entities/ics-paris|ICS Paris]]
