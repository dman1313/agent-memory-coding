# Paul

## Basic Information

- **Type:** person
- **Role:** educator, technology coach; friend and collaborator of [[entities/dwayne-primeau|Dwayne Primeau]]

## Description

Paul is an educator and tech coach who was an early conversation partner for [[concepts/human-good-principle|Human Good AI]]'s founding ideas. During a bike ride with [[entities/dwayne-primeau|Dwayne Primeau]], Paul raised the concept of using AI for human good — a conversation that partly inspired the name and direction of the organization. He and Dwayne discussed how AI could support schools practically. His surname is not recorded in available sources.
