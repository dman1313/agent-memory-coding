# John Schatzky

## Basic Information

- **Type:** person
- **Role:** educator; former head of Canadian Academy; collaborator on education-focused AI ideas

## Description

John Schatzky is an educator and former head of [[entities/canadian-academy|Canadian Academy]] (Osaka). He has been in conversations with [[entities/dwayne-primeau|Dwayne Primeau]] about using AI to support overwhelmed teachers in Canada. His framing — "give back to society" rather than maximize profit — helped shape the mission of [[concepts/human-good-principle|Human Good AI]]. He is working on projects to support Canadian schools.
