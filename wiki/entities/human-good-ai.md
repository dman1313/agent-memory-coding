# Human Good AI

## Basic Information

- **Type:** organization (startup / social enterprise)
- **Founder:** [[entities/dwayne-primeau|Dwayne Primeau]]
- **Stage:** early — solo founder, products in trial

## Description

Human Good AI is a startup founded by [[entities/dwayne-primeau|Dwayne Primeau]] with the mission of using AI agents to support nonprofits, charter schools, and mission-driven organizations — freeing humans from back-office admin so they can focus on meaningful work. The founding principle: "less admin, more good." First product is [[entities/agent-ready|Agent Ready]], a Cloudflare Worker layer that makes websites agent-friendly. A longer-term goal is an "office in a box" for schools and nonprofits. Financially, Human Good plans to launch paid products first to fund goodwill/impact projects, alongside grant and token-donation fundraising. As of 2026-06, it is a solo operation.
