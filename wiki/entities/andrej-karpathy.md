# Andrej Karpathy

## Basic Information
- Type: person
- Source: [[sources/jeff-company-brain-vault-explainer|Building a Company Knowledge Vault]]

## Description
Andrej Karpathy is credited as the originator of the [[concepts/llm-curated-wiki|LLM-curated wiki]] approach this vault is built on — using structured, human-readable wiki articles maintained by a language model in place of vector embeddings. The company-vault explainer borrows his principle directly as its core design choice.
