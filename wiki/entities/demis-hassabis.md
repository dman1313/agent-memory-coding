# Demis Hassabis

## Basic Information

- **Type:** person
- **Role:** co-founder and CEO of DeepMind / Google DeepMind; cited as inspiration for Human Good AI

## Description

Demis Hassabis is the CEO of Google DeepMind, widely known for directing AI research toward solving major scientific problems (AlphaFold, drug discovery). He is cited by [[entities/dwayne-primeau|Dwayne Primeau]] as an inspiration: a video about Hassabis and his stated goal of directing AI toward human benefit sparked early thinking about [[concepts/human-good-principle|Human Good AI]]. Hassabis is not directly affiliated with Human Good AI.
