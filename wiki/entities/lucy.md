# Lucy

## Basic Information

- **Type:** person
- **Role:** art teacher at [[entities/ics-paris|ICS Paris]]

## Description

Lucy is the art specialist (atelierista/art teacher) at [[entities/ics-paris|ICS Paris]]. She is a recurring subject of concern in the school transcripts: her resistance to collaboration with homeroom teachers, reluctance to attend planning meetings (CPMs), failure to share lesson plans in advance, and disputes over classroom logistics (location of art classes, use of a cart, cleanup responsibilities) are documented across multiple late 2024 voice notes. She claimed a handicap status (via CSE) which factored into decisions about where she taught. [[entities/dwayne-primeau|Dwayne Primeau]] and other staff developed a formal plan of essential agreements and coaching steps to address the issues.
