# Dwayne Primeau

## Basic Information

- **Type:** person
- **Roles:** founder of [[concepts/human-good-principle|Human Good AI]]; educator; former principal/head of school

## Description

Dwayne Primeau is the founder of [[concepts/human-good-principle|Human Good AI]] and an educator with a background as a principal and head of school. He worked in international education (IB schools), including a nonprofit school in Osaka and later ICS Paris / Alpha Boulevard. He describes himself as a self-identified "early adopter" and "agentic engineer" — someone who actively builds with AI agent frameworks. The Human Good project emerged from his experience seeing how much time and money schools and nonprofits spend on back-office operations that could be automated. He is based in Paris as of 2026.
