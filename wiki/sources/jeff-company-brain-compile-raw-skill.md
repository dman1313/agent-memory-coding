## Core Content
This source is the original `compile-raw` skill definition for turning raw inputs into a well-linked wiki — the basis for this vault's [[concepts/compile-raw|compile-raw]] procedure (now generalized as the `compile` mode in `schema/curate-modes.md`). It specifies finding unprocessed files, extracting people/companies/projects/concepts selectively, pulling claims and decisions as direct quotes, and creating or appending dated sections with aggressive `[[wikilinks]]`. The original targets a `topics/people/companies/projects` taxonomy; this vault instead uses the [[concepts/Wiki-Structure|entities/concepts/sources]] structure, so only the *method* is adopted, not its folder layout.

## Main Points
- **Selectivity**: "A name mentioned once in passing is not an entity worth a wiki article."
- **Links are mandatory**: "An article with zero outbound links is a failure."
- **Minimize interpretation**: "Lean on direct quotes for strong claims."
- **Flag, do not merge**: surface overlaps for human decision rather than auto-merging.
